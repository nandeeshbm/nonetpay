Why NONETPAY

Built for the Real World

Where networks fail, NONETPAY keeps transactions flowing — instantly, securely, without data.

100% Offline Payments

Generate and receive signed vouchers with zero internet. Balance is cached locally and synced the moment connectivity returns.

Cryptographic Security

Every voucher signed with ECDSA secp256k1 — same cryptography as Bitcoin. Impossible to forge, tamper with, or replay.

QR Code Payments

Scan merchant QR to pay instantly. Works even with airplane mode on both devices — no NFC, no Bluetooth required.

Auto Background Sync

Transactions queue locally and sync automatically the moment internet is restored — no manual action from user or merchant.

No Double Spend

Each voucher has a unique cryptographic ID. Server rejects duplicates — even if the same voucher is presented multiple times offline.

AI Spending Insights

Built-in Gemini AI analyzes spending patterns, gives personalized financial tips, and answers queries in plain natural language.

The Flow

How It Works

Four steps. Entirely offline. End-to-end cryptographic integrity guaranteed.

1

Add Balance Online (Once)

Top up your NONETPAY wallet via Razorpay — UPI, debit/credit cards supported. Balance is encrypted and cached on your device, ready for offline use anytime.

2

User Scans Merchant QR — No Internet

Open NONETPAY, tap Pay, scan the merchant's static QR code. The app generates a unique ECDSA-signed payment voucher and deducts balance locally — entirely offline.

3

Merchant Scans the Voucher QR

User shows the signed voucher QR. Merchant scans and stores it offline. When either party reconnects, the voucher is automatically submitted and queued for settlement.

Server Verifies & Settles Instantly

Backend verifies the ECDSA signature, checks for duplicates, deducts from user balance, and credits the merchant — guaranteeing end-to-end integrity.
